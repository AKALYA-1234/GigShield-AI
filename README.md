# 🚀 GigShield AI

### AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Workers

---

## 📌 Overview

GigShield AI is an intelligent, AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect gig economy delivery workers (Swiggy, Zomato, Zepto, etc.) from income loss caused by external disruptions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, pollution, or curfews.

Unlike traditional insurance systems, GigShield AI is:

* **Predictive** – anticipates disruptions before they happen
* **Preventive** – suggests safer work zones to avoid income loss
* **Autonomous** – executes instant payouts without manual claims

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Gig workers depend on daily earnings. External disruptions like weather or environmental conditions can reduce their working hours and cause **20–30% income loss**, with no financial safety net.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

GigShield AI provides a **fully automated parametric insurance system** that:

* Predicts disruptions using AI
* Dynamically adjusts weekly premiums
* Detects real-time disruptions
* Prevents loss via smart zone recommendations
* Automatically compensates lost income

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🔮 1. Predictive Risk Engine

* Forecasts disruptions for the next 1–3 hours
* Estimates potential income loss

---

### 💸 5. Zero-Touch Auto Payout

### 📊 7. Intelligent Dashboard

* Real-time risk tracking

---

## 💰 Weekly Pricing Model

Premium is dynamically calculated based on:

Weekly Premium = Daily Earnings × 0.8% × Risk Multiplier

* Low Risk → Lower premium
* High Risk → Higher premium

---

## ⚙️ System Workflow

1. User registers with earnings & location
2. AI predicts future risk
3. System monitors weather + activity
4. Suggests safer zones (loss prevention)
5. If disruption occurs → auto claim triggered
6. Fraud checks applied
7. Instant payout credited
